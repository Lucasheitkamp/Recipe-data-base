Oliebollen Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 ½ teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 1 cup lukewarm milk
  • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 apple, peeled and diced
  • 1/2 cup raisins
  • Oil, for frying
  • Powdered sugar, for dusting
`

How to make Oliebollen

  1. In a small bowl, dissolve the yeast and sugar in lukewarm milk. Let it sit for about 5 minutes until it becomes frothy.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour and salt. Make a well in the center and pour in the yeast mixture. Mix it well until you have a smooth batter.
  3. Add the egg and vanilla extract to the batter, and continue mixing until well combined.
  4. Fold in the diced apple and raisins into the batter. Stir gently until they are evenly distributed.
  5. Cover the bowl with a clean kitchen towel and let the batter rise in a warm place for about 1 hour or until it has doubled in size.
  6. Heat oil in a deep fryer or a large, deep pot to about 350°F (180°C).
  7. Using a spoon or a small ice cream scoop, carefully drop portions of the batter into the hot oil. Fry them in batches, making sure not to overcrowd the pot.
  8. Cook the oliebollen for about 4-5 minutes, flipping them occasionally, until they turn golden brown and cooked through.
  9. Using a slotted spoon, remove the oliebollen from the oil and transfer them to a plate lined with paper towels to drain the excess oil.
  10. Repeat the frying process with the remaining batter until all the oliebollen are cooked.
  11. Once the oliebollen have cooled slightly, dust them generously with powdered sugar.

Enjoy!
